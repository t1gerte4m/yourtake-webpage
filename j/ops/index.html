  • Ops does not send anything without your approval. You're always in control.
  • Ops does not do estimating, invoicing, or bookkeeping.
  • Ops does not replace your project management tool. It's a personal assistant, not a PM app.
  • Ops does not listen to your phone calls. Only texts, emails, and what you dictate.
