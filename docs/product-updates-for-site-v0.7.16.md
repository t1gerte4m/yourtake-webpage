# YourTake Product Updates for Website (v0.7.8 through v0.7.16)

The website currently reflects roughly v0.6.7 features. The product is now at v0.7.16. The following major capabilities have shipped and need to be represented on the site.

## New Capabilities (not on site at all)

### Long-Form Content Creation
YourTake now creates full newsletters and blog posts, not just LinkedIn posts.

**Newsletters (11 styles):** 6 ways to start: from a topic, from trending content, from content gaps, from your notes, from your best recent posts, or as a case study. The system researches your topic, generates angle variants to choose from, then builds a structured outline you can edit before it writes. Drag sections, revise, pick your style, generate. 11 styles including Roundup, Narrative, Tips, How-To, Listicle, Common Mistakes, Reasons Why, Myth Busters, Case Study, One Big Idea, and Quick Take. Full editor with subject line, preview text, body, and CTA. Export to your email platform of choice.

**Blog Posts (5 styles):** Same research-backed pipeline, tuned for web content. SEO-aware title, meta description, suggested tags, structured post. Styles: Thought Leadership, How-To Guide, Listicle, Newsjacking, Case Study. Copy as Markdown or plain text for any publishing platform.

**Newsletter is also a format option in every Create workflow**, not just the dedicated Long-Form flow. Any topic or source can become a newsletter.

### Customer Voice
One-click audience research per ICP. The system automatically researches your actual audience to understand how they talk about their problems, then uses their language in your posts. Surfaces awareness levels, psychographic themes, desire ladders, and motivation layers. Integrated into all content creation workflows and campaign theme generation. Keeps content ICP-targeted without manual persona work.

### Make a Meme
AI-generated meme images with LinkedIn captions. Two modes: AI Original (custom image generation) and classic meme templates. Concept discovery via web research, multiple sorting options (Best Match, Most Timely, Spiciest, Most Relatable), and revision support. Built for B2B humor on LinkedIn.

## Updated Capabilities (on site but outdated)

### Voice Profile Building
The site describes building voice profiles from exemplar posts. The experience is now significantly better:
- Start from your **LinkedIn URL, website URL, or both**
- Deeper automatic scraping of your existing content
- **Conversational gap-fill**: the system asks smart follow-up questions with pre-populated answers and tone/POV chips instead of a generic form
- **Post-build summary** with an auto-generated exemplar post showing how the voice sounds before you use it
- **Import existing AI voice work** (e.g., from Claude or ChatGPT voice guides)

### Multi-Profile & Executive Voice
The site mentions per-client voice profiles for agencies and executive profiles for teams. This now includes:
- **Executive voice overlay**: company base layer + individual executive voice on top
- **Profile switching** works seamlessly between profiles
- **Voice delta summaries** showing what makes each profile distinct

### Campaign Planner
Now integrates Customer Voice data into theme generation. Campaigns are automatically targeted to your audience's language and awareness levels, not just your content strategy.

### Content Formats
The site lists 4 formats (text, carousel, video script, infographic). The actual count is now **7**: text posts, carousels, video scripts (6 styles), infographics, **newsletters, blog posts, and memes**.
